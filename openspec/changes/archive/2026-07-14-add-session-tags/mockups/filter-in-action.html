Empty & clear states

No chips selected → tag axis is inert (shows all). One click on a selected chip deselects it; a "clear tags" affordance appears only while ≥1 tag is active. Selecting a chip that matches zero sessions shows an inline "0 match" rather than an empty list with no explanation.
